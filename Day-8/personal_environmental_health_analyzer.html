Most Surprising Finding
Despite June being a pre-monsoon month, Delhi's AQI (82) is notably better than Kolkata's (133) — usually it's the reverse. Kolkata's high humidity combined with industrial emissions creates a pollution trap exceeding even Delhi's levels this month.
Key Trend
North Indian cities (Ghaziabad, Delhi) have a 3.2× higher AQI than South Indian cities (Bangalore, Chennai) due to geography, industry density, and monsoon patterns. India's PM2.5 was 48.9 µg/m³ in 2025 — nearly 10× the WHO guideline.

Personalized Recommendations — Ghaziabad, UP
Daily Actions
Check AQI every morning before going out via aqi.in or IQAir
Wear N95/KN95 mask when AQI exceeds 100
Avoid peak traffic hours (7–10 AM, 6–9 PM) for outdoor activities
Keep windows closed during daytime; ventilate only at night
Drink filtered/RO water; avoid tap water directly
Indoor Air Improvement
Install HEPA air purifier — essential for Ghaziabad's AQI 152
Add indoor plants: Snake plant, Peace Lily, Spider plant
Use exhaust fans while cooking to remove PM2.5 from cooking fumes
Vacuum carpets and upholstery twice weekly with HEPA vacuum
Use beeswax candles or salt lamps — avoid synthetic air fresheners
Outdoor Activity Guidance
Exercise only when AQI is below 100 — currently unsafe for outdoor workouts
Prefer indoor gyms, yoga studios, or home workouts
If outdoors, stay away from main roads and industrial areas
Best time if needed: early morning (5–6 AM) when AQI dips slightly
Hair Care — Hard Water
Install a shower filter — Ghaziabad TDS can reach 975 mg/L
Use sulfate-free, moisturizing shampoo; avoid daily washing
Do weekly apple cider vinegar rinse to remove mineral buildup
Apply deep conditioning mask (argan or coconut oil) twice a week
Consider Biotin + Zinc supplements for pollution-related hair fall
Skin Care — Pollution Shield
Double-cleanse every evening to remove PM2.5 particles from skin
Use antioxidant-rich Vitamin C serum daily — neutralizes free radicals
Apply broad-spectrum SPF 50+ sunscreen; UV + pollution combo worsens aging
Use a barrier cream with ceramides to protect against dry, polluted air
Drink 3+ liters water daily to compensate for dehydrating hard water
Water Quality Fixes
Install RO + UV purifier — mandatory for Ghaziabad groundwater
Use water softener system for bathing if TDS above 500 mg/L
Collect and test tap water annually via CPCB-approved labs
Prefer bottled water for drinking if RO system unavailable
