Comment on 5 posts in your target space

›

The Value Comment formula: Agree + Add Insight + Ask a Question.

Example: "Agreed that clean data matters more than model choice for most NLP tasks. I ran into this myself trying to clean text data for a small project — curious how you handle inconsistent formatting at scale?"

This gets you visible to the poster's audience — often other developers and recruiters — without pitching yourself directly.

›
Something I learned building my first NLP project: the hardest part usually isn't the model — it's the data prep before it. ~380 characters. Ends on a question to drive comments.
6

Engage + send 5 more connection requests

›

Reply to every comment on both posts — even a short "thanks, agreed!" keeps the post alive in others' feeds. Then send 5 more targeted requests using the Day 3 template, this time to people at companies actively posting "we're hiring" content.

7

Review the numbers

›

Profile views up? Your headline/keyword changes are working — you're showing up in more searches.

Connection acceptance rate low (under ~30%)? Your requests may be too generic or targeting the wrong people — tighten who you're messaging.

Post impressions low? Try posting at a different time, or lead with a stronger hook line — the first sentence is doing most of the work.

Whatever the numbers say, repeat this cycle weekly. LinkedIn rewards consistency more than any single "perfect" post.
