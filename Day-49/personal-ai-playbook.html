    What is this?

    A plain-language walkthrough of the whole app.

    Personal AI Playbook is a personal library of reusable AI prompts, tailored to your work as a software engineer focused on debugging and code review. It's built so you never have to remember or rewrite a good prompt from scratch — you fill in a few fields and copy the result into Claude (or any AI tool).

    01

    Dashboard

    Your home base. Shows your favorited workflows and quick links into the two builders.

    02

    Debugging Workflows & Code Review Workflows

    Ready-made, editable prompt templates organized by what you actually use AI for. Open one, fill in the blanks (paste your error, choose your language, etc.), and copy the finished prompt.

    03

    Prompt Builder

    For when no template fits. Add "building blocks" like Role, Objective, or Constraints, pick a preset or write your own text for each, and a complete prompt assembles itself in the live preview.

    04

    Loop Builder

    Wraps any prompt with instructions to keep retrying on its own — with a clear goal, a way to judge each attempt, and rules for when to stop — instead of a one-shot answer.

    05

    My Workflows

    Everything you've saved lives here. Search, filter, favorite, duplicate, edit, or delete any workflow. Everything is stored locally in this browser.

    Welcome to your AI Playbook

    Built around how you debug and review code with Claude.

    This is a local, private library of AI prompt systems — no account, nothing sent to a server. Three things to know before you dive in:

    →

    Everything is editable

    Every template, including the built-in ones, is just a starting point. Tweak it, duplicate it, or build your own from blocks.

    →

    Start with Debugging Workflows

    Since slow, unclear debugging is your biggest bottleneck, that's where the highest-leverage templates live.

    →

    Stuck on how to phrase something?

    Open the Prompt Builder — every block explains what it does and why it matters, right where you pick it.
