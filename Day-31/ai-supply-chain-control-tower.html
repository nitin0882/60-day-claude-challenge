How the Control Tower Works

Objective

Keep your operational KPIs as high as possible (and cost as low as possible) over a 3-minute shift by resolving incoming alerts.

Alerts

Each alert shows a priority (Critical / High / Medium), a countdown, and a description. If the countdown hits zero before you act, it auto-escalates and hurts your KPIs.

Actions

  • Best-fit actions (e.g. Use Backup Supplier for a supplier delay) give the strongest KPI boost.
  • Partial-fit actions (e.g. Approve Air Freight for anything) help a little but cost more.
  • Ignore guarantees KPI damage.
  • Delay Decision buys 10 seconds but the alert gets worse while you wait.

Difficulty

Alerts spawn faster and stack up as the clock runs down. Late-game, expect multiple critical issues at once.

Scoring

You get a grade from A+ to D based on your final score, driven by correct decisions and end-of-shift KPI health.
